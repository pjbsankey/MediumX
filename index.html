ParssonDAO

Creators of Elastix

Currency Independent of the Federal Reserve

Parsson DAO is a DAO making monetary experiments to generate better currency for everyone. Instead of being subject to Federal Reserve policy, anyone who holds our governance token Parsson (PSN) gets a say on whether to expand or contract the Elastix (ELX) currency supply.

Maybe Bitcoin is the ultimate money, but just in case it isn't we want to explore alternatives. Starting with Elastix.

Elastix is a monetary experiment asking the question, do we need a dynamic, adjustable supply currency to reflect the dynamic ever changing world we live in?

JOIN US and lets find out!
